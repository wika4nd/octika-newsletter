Opticians: Why sell private label frames in your optical store?

Although private label frames have long suffered from stigma due to low quality manufacturing and lack-of-choice, which has relegated them to the rank of second-zone citizen compared to national-brand eyewear, the last few years have seen a tremendous development of offerings that has made private-label eyewear a of entry-level collections.

Private-label offerings now come with a of quality levels, design styles, customizations and price ranges that are in line with the expectations of modern-day customers that value quality and fashion just as much as price.

With the new law regarding full health insurance coverage for eyewear, it has become impossible to meet the constraints with national level suppliers in an economically viable way, but private label offerings allow you to not only comply with the requirements of the law, but also keep high profit margins. You can even afford to offer a 2nd pair for free program without incurring the high costs typically associated with this practice.
